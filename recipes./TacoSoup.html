Taco Soup Recipe Ingredients :

  • Pinto Beans
  • Black Beans
  • Lean Ground Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ranch

Taco Soup Recipe Step by Step:

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink, breaking it into crumbles; drain
  2. Add taco seasoning and mix well
  3. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  4. Bring to a boil
  5. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
